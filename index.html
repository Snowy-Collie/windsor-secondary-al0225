Alex's Future Plan 鄭張力的未來計畫

My 10-Year Plan 我的十年計畫

Over the next 10 years, I aim to successfully graduate from Windsor Secondary School and then from a university in Canada. My goal is to secure a fulfilling job here. 在接下來的十年，我計畫成功從Windsor中學畢業，然後進入加拿大一所大學就讀，並最終在這裡找到一份有意義的工作。

College Choice 大學選擇

I aspire to attend either the University of British Columbia (UBC) or the University of Alberta, as I’ve heard their computer science programs are exceptional. I am passionate about studying engineering, software, or hardware, fields that align with my interests and career goals. 我希望能進入不列顛哥倫比亞大學(UBC)或阿爾伯塔省大學，因為我聽說那裡的計算機科學專業非常出色。我熱衷於學習工程、軟件或硬件相關專業，這些領域與我的興趣和職業目標高度契合。

Career Choice 職業選擇

Upon entering the workforce, I aspire to become a software engineer or hardware designer. I am particularly interested in developing aviation-themed games, similar to my current project, ACSRS: Rewriting the Sky, which combines my passion for technology and aviation history. 當我進入職場後，我希望成為軟件工程師或硬件設計師。我特別有興趣開發與航空主題相關的遊戲，類似於我目前正在製作的《ACSRS：重寫天空》，這個項目結合了我對技術和航空歷史的熱情。

Personal Life 個人生活

I plan to build my career in Canada because I find the environment here comfortable and enjoyable, especially with the opportunity to enjoy winter activities like playing in the snow. Additionally, I look forward to creating engaging and fun games, as mentioned earlier, to share my passion with others. 我計畫在加拿大發展我的職業，因為我覺得這裡的環境舒適且宜人，特別是冬天可以玩雪的機會。同時，我期待製作有趣的遊戲，與他人分享我的熱情，正如前面所述。
